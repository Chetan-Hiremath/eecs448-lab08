This profile is about Mario. Mario is video game character of Nintendo. He is an Italian plumber of the Mushroom Kingdom, and he is the main protagonist in his games. He saves Princess Peach of the Mushroom Kingdom when Bowser, the main antagonist, kindaps her. He gets help from allies like Luigi, who is Mario's young brother, Toads, who are Princess Peach's mushroom-like servants, and Yoshi, who is a green dinosaur. Mario uses power-ups like fire flower, ice flower, tanooki leaf, mushroom, and acorn to fight Bowser's enemies. When Mario defeats Bowser, he resuces Princess Peach. Then, he is considered as a hero in the Mushroom Kingdom. He has several video games like Super Mario, Mario Kart, Super Smash Bros, Mario Party, and Super Mario 3D. He is one of most popular characters in the video game franchise, and he is the mascot of Nintendo. Nintendo is not strong without Mario since he is the most important character in Nintendo.
